Back Story

Deadpool is initially depicted as a supervillain of the New Mutants and X-Force, though later stories would portray him as an antihero. Deadpool is the alter ego of Wade Wilson, a disfigured Canadian mercenary with superhuman regenerative healing abilities.

Power Origins

Wade Wilson received a serum injection to activate his supposedly dormant mutant genes. To catalyze the serum, Wade accepts torture to secrete adrenaline in his body, and the serum works on his body, helping his cells regenrate at a rapid speed.

Deadpool's Weakness

The fast-talking gunman seems almost fearless on the battlefield, but in reality, there's a lot that scares the Merc with the Mouth. There are two things though, that scare him above all: cows and clowns are a Deadpool weakness.
